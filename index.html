IMEI Generator ("us", "we", or "our") operates the IMEI Generator Chrome extension (the "Service").

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from users of the Service. The Service operates entirely within the user's browser and does not transmit any data to external servers or third parties.

Permissions

The Service requires the following permissions to function:

  • activeTab: Allows the extension to access the current tab to execute the IMEI generation and copying process.
  • clipboardWrite: Enables the extension to copy the generated IMEI numbers directly to the user's clipboard.
  • contextMenus: Used to create custom context menu options for selecting device models.
  • storage: Utilized to save the user's selected device model between sessions.

These permissions are solely used to provide the intended functionality of the Service and are not used for any other purpose.
